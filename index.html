INFOSYS-Cambridge AI Centre Hackathon

Learn more


We are pleased to announce the INFOSYS Cambridge AI Centre hackathon, that will take place at West Hub on 12th May 2025! We welcome all students in natural sciences, mathematics, computer science, engineering, and related disciplines to get together for 1 day of problem solving on the topic of ... . During this day, participants will compete in teams of 3-5 people to find an innovative way of ... . Participants will be provided pre-recorded data from fNIRS and fMRI, and each team will need to ... . Projects will be evaluated by judges, experts in their fields, and the winning team will share a £... prize. In addition, teams will also compete for the people's choice prize of £..., voted for by all the participants. Food, coffee and tea will be provided throughout the day.

We are hoping this event will contribute to building a community of people working in Data Intensive Sciences around Cambridge, and foster the sharing of practices between the different related research fields!
